What You Receive

📸 Professional Albums

Beautiful magazine-style albums printed on premium paper with your favorite moments thoughtfully curated. Each spread is designed to tell your wedding story with artistic layouts and high-quality printing. Albums vary from 15-30 spreads depending on your package.

🖼️ Premium Enlargements

Professionally printed large-format enlargements (16x24, 20x30, or custom sizes) perfect for displaying in your home. These museum-quality prints come with protective coating and can be framed for a stunning display of your favorite moments.

📱 Digital Images

Receive all your professionally edited high-resolution images delivered on premium USB and via secure cloud link. Platinum & Luxury packages include 200+ edited images, plus complete unedited RAW files for your personal archival.

💌 Thank You Cards

100 personalized thank you cards printed with your chosen wedding photo. These beautifully designed cards help you express gratitude to your guests in a memorable and personal way.

🎬 Live Streams & Highlights

Mini mobile streams allow family and friends to watch the live action in real-time. Pre-shoot slide shows can be displayed during your wedding reception. Professional highlight videos available as add-ons.

🎨 Signature Board (Luxury Only)

An exclusive 16x24 signature board featuring your most stunning moment. This premium print serves as a beautiful keepsake and can be displayed during reception for guest signatures.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I customize the hours or add more coverage?
Absolutely! All packages are flexible. We can extend coverage hours, add extra pre-shoots, or include additional events like engagement shoots or reception-only coverage. Contact us for custom pricing.
What's included in the unedited images?
You receive complete RAW files for all shots taken during your wedding. These are unedited originals that allow you to have backups and make any personal edits you prefer.
How long does it take to receive the final images?
Typically 4-6 weeks from your wedding date. This allows us to carefully edit every image to our high standards. Urgent requests may be accommodated for an additional fee.
Can I order additional prints after delivery?
Yes! You'll have lifetime access to reorder prints, additional albums, or enlargements at competitive pricing. We keep all your images archived securely.
What's the difference between album spreads?
A spread is two facing pages. A 20-spread album contains 40 pages of beautifully designed layouts with your photos. More spreads mean more images and larger format pages.
What payment and deposit are required?
A 30% advance payment is required to secure your date. The remaining balance is due one week before your wedding. We accept multiple payment methods for your convenience.
Do you offer videography or highlight reels?
Photography is our specialty, but we can recommend trusted videography partners. Highlight reels and cinematic videos are available as premium add-ons to any package.
What if I need additional photographers?
Each package includes three-camera coverage. For larger venues or split events, we can add assistant photographers. Large format events receive dedicated crew planning.
